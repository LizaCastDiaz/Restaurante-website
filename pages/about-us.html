Our Story

Situated on the famous ‘Pho Mile’ in the heart of Shoreditch, Viet Grill serves authentic Vietnamese street food from Vietnamese Phở to Bún Chả Hà Nội, and sharing plates to flavour-packed grilled dishes. Be transported to the heart of Saigon with laid back interiors alongside a traditional menu using fresh and locally sourced ingredients. From BBQ honey coated ribs to grilled beef in a sweet soy marinade, we pride ourselves on our dishes cooked over charcoal on our fire burning josper grills!
